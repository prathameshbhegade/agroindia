The soil should be friable, porous and well drained and optimum pH range is 5.5 to 7.5. Temperature range between 20°C to 50 °C is ideal and rainfall 750 – 1200 mm per annum.

Sugarcane plantations like Adsali, Pre-Seasonal, Suru, Ratoon are done in various seasons

Co.86032 (Neera), Co. M. 0265 (Phule-265), Co. V.S.I. 9805 (Sharad-1), V.S.I.434

Adsali plantation needs NPK in the proportion 160:68:68 per acre. Fertilizer quantities will vary as per soil test report and as per required yield. Apply fertilizers in split doses. As per growth stages apply growth hormones e.g Calcium Nitrate. In case of drip irrigation method, recommended fertilizers must be given at regular intervals.

Aphids, Whiteflies and Mealybugs are the major sucking pests in Sugarcane. Stem borer, shoot borer and White Grubs infestation is also observed in sugarcane field. Bore holes at the top of the shoot, a bunchy top appearance, Dead heart are the major symptoms of borer pest. Management -Foliar spray of Beauveria bassiana, Metarhizium and Vetricillium as preventative sprays and Dimethoate 30% EC Or Fipronil 5% SC Or Chlorpyriphos 30 EC Or Chlorantraniliprole 18.5 SC must be done.

After 480 to 500 days:Harvesting must be done considering the maturity indices. Skilled workers or mechanical harvesting can be done for harvesting sugarcane. Average Yield- 60-80 /acre
