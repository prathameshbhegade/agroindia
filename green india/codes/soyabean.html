1 to 1 Soyabean crop requires NPK and S at the rate of 12, 28, 12 and 8 kg/acre. Use the NPK fertilizers as per the soil test report.

Different types of caterpillars like Tobacco leaf eating caterpillar (Spodoptera), Semilooper, Ghateali and stem pest like Stem fly and Girdle beetle etc. feed on soybean crop from initial growth stages of crop. Sucking pest complex like aphids, jassids and white flies attack on soybean. Girdle beetle and stem fly- feed on the stem and kill the plants. These pest damages are more in the seedling stage of crops. Do soil application of Metarhizium spp,Verticillium spp, Beauveria spp. through irrigation water. For sucking pest complex spray Neem oil, Beauveria Bassiana, Verticillium lecanii, Metarhizium anisopliae. Spray any of one below insecticide in case of heavy infestation : Quinalphos 25 EC or Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC or Diflubenzuron 25% EC or Indoxacarb 14.5 SC at weekly intervals.

Soyabean should be harvested, when leaves start falling and pods look dry, but before getting completely dried. Post harvesting, threshing is usually done by beating pods with sticks or using mechanical threshing. Average yield- 8-10 qtl/acre
